## Description (optimized for AMO search)

**Save money automatically while you shop online — CouponSnap finds and applies coupon codes for you!**

CouponSnap is the smartest coupon add-on for Firefox. When you reach a checkout page, CouponSnap automatically detects the coupon field, tries every available promo code, and applies the best one — completely hands-free.

✂️ **HOW IT WORKS**
1. Shop normally at your favorite store
2. When you reach checkout, CouponSnap detects it automatically
3. CouponSnap tests all available coupon codes in seconds
4. The best working code is applied — you see your savings instantly!

• Amazon • Walmart • Target • eBay • Best Buy

💰 **KEY FEATURES**
• 🤖 Automatic coupon detection — no clicking needed
• ⚡ Tests multiple codes in seconds
• 🛒 Works on Shopify, WooCommerce, and major retail sites
• 🏷️ 390+ coupon codes for 50+ stores, updated regularly
• 📱 Clean popup shows real-time savings status
• 🔒 Privacy-first — no account needed, no data collected
• 💚 Completely free forever

🔐 **PRIVACY & SECURITY**
CouponSnap does NOT:
• Collect or sell your personal data
• Track your browsing history
• Require account creation
• Send data to external servers

All coupon data is bundled locally in the extension. CouponSnap only activates on checkout pages — it never runs in the background watching your browsing.
