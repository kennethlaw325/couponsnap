## Full Description (optimized for ASO)

**Save money automatically while you shop online — CouponSnap finds and applies coupon codes for you!**

CouponSnap is the smartest coupon extension for Chrome. When you're on a checkout page, CouponSnap automatically detects the coupon field, tries every available promo code, and applies the best one — all without you lifting a finger.

✂️ **HOW IT WORKS**
1. Shop normally at your favorite store
2. When you reach checkout, CouponSnap detects it automatically
3. CouponSnap tries all available coupon codes in seconds
4. The best working code is applied — you see your savings instantly!

CouponSnap recognizes checkout pages automatically using URL patterns and page structure analysis. It works on standard checkout flows as well as Shopify, WooCommerce, and custom checkout pages.
