✂️ CouponSnap Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2026

TL;DR: CouponSnap does not collect, store, or sell any personal data. All coupon data is stored locally in the extension. We do not have servers that receive your browsing data.

1. Overview

CouponSnap ("we", "our", or "us") is a Chrome browser extension that automatically finds and applies coupon codes while you shop online. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use CouponSnap.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

CouponSnap does not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, address, etc.)
  • Browsing history or URLs visited
  • Purchase history or transaction data
  • Credit card or payment information
  • Cookies or session data
  • Geolocation data
  • Any analytics or telemetry data

3. How CouponSnap Works

CouponSnap operates entirely within your browser:

  • Coupon codes are bundled inside the extension — no external API calls are made to fetch coupons
  • Checkout page detection is performed locally by analyzing the current page URL and DOM structure
  • Coupon codes are applied directly to the checkout form on the page you're visiting
  • No data about your shopping session is transmitted to our servers

4. Permissions Explained

CouponSnap requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • activeTab: Required to interact with the current tab's checkout page to apply coupon codes
  • storage: Used to store your extension preferences locally on your device only
  • scripting: Required to inject coupon-finding code into checkout pages
  • contextMenus: Adds a right-click option to manually trigger coupon search

CouponSnap does not request permission to read all browsing history, access other tabs, or communicate with external servers.

5. Affiliate Disclosures

CouponSnap may modify product links to include affiliate tracking parameters (e.g., Amazon Associates tag). This allows us to earn a small commission when you make a purchase — at no additional cost to you. This is how we keep CouponSnap free.

Affiliate commissions do not affect which coupon codes we show you or the order in which they are tried.

6. Third-Party Services

CouponSnap does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

7. Children's Privacy

CouponSnap is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of CouponSnap after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
